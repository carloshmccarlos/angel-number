## Design Context

### Users
* **Spiritual Explorers & Meaning Searchers**: Adults aged 25-45, primarily in English-speaking markets (and later global). They frequently see repeating numbers (e.g., 111, 444) in daily life and search for their symbolic significance.
* **Context**: Often accessing the app on a mobile device while reflecting, looking for quick, personalized, and visually captivating validation.
* **Jobs to be Done**:
  1. Calculate name/birthday numerology vibrations quickly.
  2. Read high-quality, personalized symbolic explanations.
  3. Feel a sense of deep personal resonance and reflection.

### Brand Personality
* **3-Word Voice**: Ethereal, Reflective, Precise.
* **Emotional Goals**: Wonder, self-awareness, inner calm, and intellectual interest (rooted in historical numerology, not superstitious hype).
* **Anti-References**: Avoid cheap psychic hotline aesthetics, cartoony clip-art, or chaotic fortune-telling layouts. It must not look like an ad-heavy spam site or a generic SaaS dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
* **Vibe & Texture Archetype**: **Ethereal Glass**
  * Deep OLED black/cosmic gradient canvas, with subtle radial mesh gradients (soft stardust, deep indigo, and muted violet/amber orbs).
  * Vantablack/glass card enclosures with `backdrop-blur-2xl` and ultra-thin pure white/10 border highlights (`border-white/10`).
  * Subliminal space dust / noise layer (`opacity-[0.02]`) for texture depth.
* **Layout Archetype**: **Asymmetrical Bento** (Result page) & **Editorial Split** (Home/Interactive screen)
  * Homepage utilizes a split layout: left side has large elegant typography with the core message; right side contains the interactive input card enclosure.
  * Result page uses an asymmetrical bento grid showing name calculation process, life path calculation process, and focus area interpretation.
* **Theme**: Pure Dark Mode (fits the night sky/cosmic vibration theme perfectly).

### Design Principles
1. **Kinetic Mysticism (Motion Choreography)**: Transitions must feel smooth, decelerating naturally (`ease-out-expo`), mimicking the floating movement of celestial bodies.
2. **Double-Bezel Enclosure**: Containers, cards, and input groups are wrapped in nested containers with custom border ratios to look like premium, machined elements.
3. **Structured Clarity**: Balance the spiritual feel with academic numerology structure (clean tabular views of Pythagorean grids, equations, and steps) to maintain high credibility.
4. **Resonating Visuals**: Avoid thick generic borders, gradient-filled text, or boring cards. Colors should be used as subtle glow accents.
